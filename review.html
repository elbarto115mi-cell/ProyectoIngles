Platon

Inspired by the "Myth of the Cave." It shows how we live deceived and how awakening to the truth can be painful but liberating.

Review:
A modern metaphor for the search for truth. Ideal for discussing freedom, knowledge, and the manipulation of power.

Aristoteles

Book: “Nichomachean Ethics”

Review:
Explores how to achieve happiness through virtue (eudaimonia). Proposes a rational ethic, applicable even today in debates about morality and bioethics.
Topics: Happiness, Morality, Justice.
